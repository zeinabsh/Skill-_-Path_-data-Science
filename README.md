# Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Analytics Data Challenge

## Overview

In this challenge, you will analyze a dataset representing a European pharmacy chain distributor operating across multiple European countries. 
The dataset includes daily sales transactions by pharmacy and product, with supporting dimensions for time, geography and product hierarchy.

## The dataset includes:
- Daily sales transactions
- Pharmacy information
- Product hierarchy
- Geographic dimensions
- Time-based transaction data


---

## Your task is to use Python and Pandas to:

- Data cleaning skills
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Business analytics thinking
- Pandas proficiency
- Data visualization skills
- Ability to communicate insights clearly


---

## Business Questions

### Sales Analysis

- How do revenue, units sold, and margin change over time?
- Are there clear seasonal patterns?

### Geographic Analysis

- Which countries and regions contribute the most to total revenue and margin?
- How does performance vary from:
  - Country
  - Region
  - Pharmacy

### Pharmacy Performance

- Which pharmacies outperform or underperform compared to others in the same region?
- How do Urban, Suburban, and Rural pharmacies differ in sales volume and profitability?

### Product Analysis

- Which product categories and brands generate the most revenue?
- Which generate the most margin?
- Are there products with:
  - High volume but low margin?
  - Low volume but high margin?

### Promotion Analysis

- How do promoted sales compare to non-promoted sales in terms of:
  - Volume
  - Revenue
  - Margin

### Regional Contribution

- How does regional performance contribute to overall business results?

### Geographic Insights

- Are there visible geographic patterns in sales or profitability when viewed on a map?
